# Master Joint Agreement

**DEMO. NOT LEGAL ADVICE.**

This Master Joint Agreement ("Agreement") governs your use of the Epimage Platform ("Platform") as a buyer of digital photography Deeds. By signing this Agreement electronically prior to your first purchase, you confirm you have read, understood, and agree to all terms.

## 1. Eligibility

1.1. You represent that you are eighteen (18) years of age or older and have legal capacity to enter binding agreements in your jurisdiction.

1.2. You represent that you are not on any OFAC, SDN, or equivalent international sanctions list, and that your jurisdiction does not prohibit the use of services like the Platform.

## 2. What you are buying

2.1. Each purchase on the Platform conveys to you a digital Deed of Ownership ("Deed") representing the unique edition (1 of 1) of a digital photograph Work ("Work"). The Deed is a non-fungible token minted on the Solana blockchain.

2.2. **The Deed is not the photograph itself.** The Deed is a cryptographic receipt of ownership. The photograph remains the copyrighted work of its creator.

2.3. Your purchase grants you, as the holder of the Deed:
  (a) The exclusive right to display, exhibit, and privately enjoy the Work in its unique-edition form;
  (b) The right to transfer the Deed to any third party (subject to Section 4 royalties);
  (c) Access to a personalized Share Copy variant of the Work for online posting;
  (d) The right to download the original file with the limitations described in Section 3.

## 3. What you are not buying

3.1. Your purchase **does not** convey:
  (a) Copyright in the Work;
  (b) The right to reproduce the Work for sale;
  (c) The right to use the Work in commercial contexts beyond the personal display rights in Section 2.3;
  (d) The right to create derivative works;
  (e) The right to authorize others to reproduce or distribute the Work.

3.2. If you download the original file of the Work, you forfeit the right to have your Deed transferred or resold through the Platform. Once the original is downloaded, the Platform no longer guarantees the integrity of the deed-asset binding.

3.3. Any reproduction, public distribution, or commercial use of the Work beyond the personal display rights in Section 2.3 constitutes copyright infringement and may subject you to legal action by the creator.

## 4. Payment, taxes, and royalties

4.1. All purchases are processed through Stripe. The Platform operates under Stripe Managed Payments and is the merchant of record. Stripe is responsible for tax calculation and collection where applicable.

4.2. Of the net sale proceeds (after Stripe fees and taxes), ninety percent (90%) is paid to the creator of the Work and ten percent (10%) is retained by the Platform.

4.3. On any subsequent resale of the Deed, ten percent (10%) of the resale proceeds is paid to the original creator as a royalty in perpetuity, enforced at the blockchain protocol level.

## 5. Settlement and resale

5.1. Following purchase, a thirty (30) day settlement period applies during which the Deed cannot be listed for resale on the Platform.

5.2. After the settlement period, you may list the Deed for resale at any price you choose, subject to the royalty obligation in Section 4.3.

5.3. The Platform does not guarantee a secondary market for any specific Deed.

## 6. Privacy and visibility of your ownership

6.1. Upon purchase, the Work transitions from public visibility (during the listing phase) to a private state. Anyone visiting the public listing URL will see a stub indicating the Work is privately owned.

6.2. You may at any time make the Work publicly visible again, in which case anyone with the link can view the Share Copy variant.

6.3. You may toggle between public and private states at any time.

## 7. No investment representation

7.1. The Platform does not represent that any Deed will appreciate in value or that any specific resale market will exist.

7.2. Deeds are sold as digital collectibles and as receipts of ownership in unique photographic editions. They are not securities, investment contracts, or pooled investment vehicles. You should not purchase Deeds with the expectation of profit derived from the efforts of others.

## 8. Identity and wallet provisioning

8.1. The Platform uses Magic Labs to manage your identity and to provision the Solana wallet that holds your Deed. By signing this Agreement, you acknowledge that Magic's terms of service apply to wallet provisioning.

8.2. The Platform does not hold the private keys to your wallet. Wallet recovery is managed by Magic Labs via your registered email address.

8.3. **If you lose access to the email account associated with your Platform Account, you may permanently lose access to your Deeds.**

## 9. Refunds

9.1. The Platform offers refunds in the following limited circumstances:
  (a) Payment was charged but the Deed mint failed;
  (b) The build pipeline failed after payment and could not be successfully retried within 24 hours;
  (c) A determination is made that the underlying Work violated the Creator Master Agreement and the listing should not have been live.

9.2. The Platform does not offer buyer-initiated refunds after the Deed has been successfully minted.

9.3. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method via Stripe.

## 10. Account closure

10.1. You may close your Account at any time. Closure of the Account does not transfer or invalidate any Deeds you hold. The Deeds remain on the Solana blockchain in your wallet.
