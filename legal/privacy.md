# Privacy Policy

**DEMO. NOT LEGAL ADVICE.**

This Privacy Policy describes how Epimage, Inc. ("Platform") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Epimage service.

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1. Information you provide

- **Account information**: When you sign in via Google OAuth (handled by Magic Labs), we receive your email address, your decentralized identifier (DID) issued by Magic, and a Solana wallet address provisioned for you by Magic.
- **Creator information**: If you become a Creator, we additionally collect your legal name, business name (if any), legal address, entity type, and tax identification number, plus optional creator-profile fields (display name, bio, YouTube channel handle, headshot photo).
- **Uploaded content**: The Works you upload, the metadata you provide about them, and ESIGN affirmations associated with them.
- **Purchase information**: When you purchase a Deed, we record your buyer wallet address, the image identifier of the Work, the purchase price, the timestamp of purchase, and the monogram text you select for the Share Copy.

### 1.2. Information from third parties

- **Stripe**: When you complete a purchase, Stripe shares with us the payment-intent identifier, the amount paid, the Stripe fee, and metadata necessary to record the transaction. Stripe also handles your card information directly. We do not see or store your card details.
- **Magic Labs**: Magic shares the wallet address provisioned for your Google-authenticated identity. Magic does not share your password.
- **Crossmint**: When an NFT is minted, Crossmint shares the mint address, transaction signature, and on-chain owner address.

### 1.3. Information collected automatically

- **Log data**: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps of access, retained for thirty (30) days for security and operational purposes.
- **Cookies**: A first-party session cookie used for authentication. We do not set third-party tracking cookies.

## 2. How we use information

We use the information described above to:

- Provide the Platform's core service (listing, purchase, deed mint, share);
- Process payments and remit taxes (via Stripe);
- Verify creator identity and screen for sanctioned individuals;
- Communicate with you about your Account (purchase confirmations, takedown notices, dispute notices);
- Investigate and respond to suspected violations of our Terms or applicable law;
- Comply with legal obligations including reporting child sexual abuse material to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).

## 3. How we share information

### 3.1. Service providers

We share information with the following service providers as necessary to operate the Platform:

- **Stripe** (payment processing and tax computation)
- **Magic Labs** (identity and wallet provisioning)
- **Crossmint** (NFT minting)
- **Cloudinary** (image hosting and delivery)
- **Arweave / ArDrive Turbo** (permanent storage of canonical Master files)

Each provider has its own privacy policy. Information shared is limited to what is necessary for the service to function.

### 3.2. On-chain disclosure

Deed metadata is published to the Solana blockchain. This metadata includes the title and description of the Work, the creator's display name, the image identifier, the buyer's wallet address, the Arweave URI of the canonical Master, perceptual and cryptographic hashes of the Work, and royalty parameters.

**Information on the Solana blockchain is public and permanent.** It cannot be deleted by us or by you.

### 3.3. Legal obligations

We will disclose information to government authorities or other third parties where required by law, including:

- Reports to NCMEC for confirmed CSAM cases;
- Responses to subpoenas, court orders, and other legal process;
- Cooperation with sanctions enforcement (OFAC, SDN).

### 3.4. Successor in interest

If Epimage, Inc. is acquired, merged, or otherwise transferred, information may be transferred to the successor entity subject to the same terms.

## 4. Data retention

- **Account data**: Retained for the life of the Account, plus three (3) years after closure for legal and tax reasons.
- **Purchase records**: Retained indefinitely for tax, audit, and on-chain reconciliation purposes.
- **Log data**: Thirty (30) days.
- **Uploaded Works (canonical Master)**: Once a Deed has been minted for a Work, the canonical Master on Arweave is permanent and cannot be deleted by the Platform.

## 5. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of personal information, subject to the legal and on-chain retention exceptions in Section 4.
- **Portability**: Request your data in a portable format.
- **Opt-out**: Opt out of certain processing.

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@epimage.com

## 6. Children's privacy

The Platform is not intended for children under thirteen (13) years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under thirteen. If you believe a child under thirteen has provided information to the Platform, contact us at privacy@epimage.com and we will delete it.

## 7. International transfers

The Platform's servers are located in the United States. By using the Platform, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect information in transit and at rest:

- TLS encryption for all client-server communication;
- AES-256-GCM encryption for canonical Master files prior to Arweave upload;
- Asymmetric encryption layer (recipient wallet pubkey) for buyer-bound Master access;
- Magic Labs' enclave-based key management for wallet private keys.

**No security system is perfect.** We cannot guarantee that information will never be accessed by unauthorized parties.

## 9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will post the updated policy to the Platform and update the version label at the top of this document. Material changes will be announced via email to registered Account holders.
