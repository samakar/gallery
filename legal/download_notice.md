# Master Download Notice

**DEMO. NOT LEGAL ADVICE.**

This Master Download Notice ("Notice") is presented to you when you, the holder of a Deed for a Work on the Epimage platform ("Platform"), request a download of the Master file underlying that Deed. By signing this Notice, you confirm you have read and understood the consequences below, and you authorize the Platform to proceed.

## 1. What "downloading the Master" means

1.1. The Master file is the original full-resolution photograph corresponding to your Deed. It is stored encrypted on Arweave; downloading it requires the Platform to release the per-image password derived from the Platform's keys, and to record the seal-break event on the Solana blockchain.

1.2. Upon successful download, the Platform will transmit (a) the encrypted Master file (or a URL to retrieve it from Arweave), and (b) the password required to extract the file using standard archive-extraction tools.

## 2. Effect on the Deed

2.1. **The Deed transitions from `sealed` to `unsealed` upon your first successful download request.** This transition is one-way and cannot be reversed. The Solana cNFT representing the Deed records this transition via an on-chain Memo published in conjunction with this download event.

2.2. **Once the Deed is `unsealed`, the Platform no longer facilitates resale or transfer of the Deed.** The Deed remains valid as a record of ownership, but the Platform's resale features will not be available for this Deed.

2.3. The Platform does not warrant the continued existence of secondary markets for `unsealed` Deeds outside the Platform. You bear the risk that no such market may exist.

## 3. Your responsibilities after download

3.1. You acknowledge that once the Master file is on a device under your control, the Platform has no technical means to retrieve, revoke, or limit further redistribution of the file.

3.2. You agree that the license terms in the Sales Agreement you previously signed continue to govern your permitted uses of the Master file, including but not limited to: restrictions on commercial reproduction, prohibitions on resale of copies, and the requirement to preserve creator attribution.

3.3. You agree to safeguard the password disclosed during this download. The password unlocks the Arweave-stored encrypted archive permanently; disclosing the password to a third party effectively grants them access to the Master file.

## 4. Acknowledgement and signature

4.1. By entering your initials below and proceeding, you confirm that:
  (a) You have read and understood this Notice;
  (b) You consent to the irreversible `sealed → unsealed` transition of your Deed;
  (c) You consent to the on-chain Memo publishing the unsealed `enc_final` value tied to your wallet;
  (d) You waive any expectation of Platform-facilitated resale or transfer of this Deed.

4.2. Your initials, the timestamp of this acknowledgement, and your wallet co-signature on the resulting Solana transaction together constitute your binding signature on this Notice.

4.3. This Notice is incorporated into the legal binder for this Deed (see binder Arweave URL on your Certificate of Authenticity). The full text above is the canonical version; the on-chain Memo carries only a hash reference back to the binder entry.
