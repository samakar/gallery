## 1. Account and identity

1.1. You represent that you are eighteen (18) years of age or older and have the legal capacity to enter into binding agreements in your jurisdiction.

1.2. You represent that all information you provide to the Platform — including your legal name, business name (if any), residential or business address, email address, and tax identification number — is true and accurate, and that you will keep it current.

1.3. You agree the Platform may perform identity verification, sanctions screening (OFAC, SDN, and equivalent international lists), and tax-reporting checks against your information.

## 2. Ownership of your work

2.1. You retain all copyright and other intellectual-property rights in the photographs you upload to the Platform ("Works"). Nothing in this Agreement transfers ownership of the Works to the Platform.

2.2. You grant the Platform a limited, non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to display, reproduce, modify (only for purposes of generating Listing Copies, Share Copies, and other operational variants), publicly perform, and distribute the Works solely as necessary to operate the Platform.

2.3. The license granted in Section 2.2 terminates upon Account closure, subject to:
  (a) the Platform's continuing right to maintain on-chain records of past transactions involving the Works;
  (b) the Platform's right to continue serving Works whose ownership has been transferred to third-party Buyers via Deeds.

## 3. Listings and edition format

3.1. Each Work you list constitutes a unique edition (1 of 1). Multi-edition listings are not supported.

3.2. You may delete a Work prior to its first sale at any time. After first sale, the Deed is non-recallable except through the takedown procedure in Section 8.

3.3. You may unlist a Work at any time prior to first sale. Once unlisted, you may edit metadata and re-list.

## 4. Compensation and royalties

4.1. Upon first sale of a Deed for a Work, you are entitled to ninety percent (90%) of the net sale proceeds after Stripe processing fees and applicable taxes are deducted.

4.2. The Platform retains ten percent (10%) of the net proceeds as a service fee.

4.3. On each subsequent secondary-market resale of a Deed, you are entitled to a ten percent (10%) royalty of the resale price, enforced at the blockchain protocol level. This royalty entitlement survives Account closure and is binding on assignees of the Deed.

4.4. Payouts are made through Stripe Connect Express on a four-week cadence following the launch of the Platform's payout infrastructure. Until that infrastructure is operational, sale proceeds are held in escrow by the Platform on your behalf.

5.1. You represent and warrant that:
  (a) You are the sole author of each Work and own all rights necessary to grant the licenses described in this Agreement;
  (b) You have obtained any model releases, location releases, property releases, and trademark or copyright clearances necessary to exhibit, sell, and distribute each Work;
  (c) The Works do not infringe the intellectual-property rights, publicity rights, or privacy rights of any third party;
  (d) The Works do not contain content prohibited by applicable law, including but not limited to child sexual abuse material (CSAM), content created with the intent of incitement to violence, or content depicting non-consensual intimate imagery.

5.2. You agree to indemnify and hold harmless the Platform from any claim arising out of breach of the representations in Section 5.1.

## 6. Moderation

6.1. The Platform conducts manual moderation review of every uploaded Work prior to listing. The Platform reserves sole discretion to reject any Work it determines, at its judgment, violates Section 5.1 or Platform community standards.

6.2. Rejections fall into two tiers: Tier 0 (content the Platform is legally required to report or remove) and Tier 1 (content violating community standards). Tier 0 rejections result in mandatory reporting to NCMEC or other applicable authorities.

6.3. Rejection of an individual Work does not entitle you to refund of any fees previously paid and does not, on its own, terminate this Agreement.

## 7. Account closure

7.1. You may close your Account at any time by giving written notice to the Platform via the email address listed in your Account.

7.2. The Platform may close your Account immediately for breach of this Agreement, including any breach of Section 5.1, or for inactivity exceeding three (3) consecutive years.

7.3. Upon closure, you remain entitled to royalties under Section 4.3 on Deeds outstanding for your Works.

## 8. Takedown and disputes

8.1. The Platform may take down any Work in response to a valid DMCA notice, court order, regulatory directive, or determination of Section 5.1 violation. Takedowns may include marking the associated Deed as "rights-disputed" or, in extreme cases, "void" on the blockchain.

8.2. State-changing actions on Deeds (including "rights-disputed", "void", and "burned" transitions) require multi-signature approval from at least three of five designated Platform signers.
